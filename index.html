Welcome to the Bandi Sanjay community!

Collaboration, community-building and connecting with State President Bandi Sanjay are the main pillars of the Bandi Sanjay App i.e. Bandi Sanjay. The Bandi Sanjay is a ‘one of its kind’ app which is powered by supporters & fans of BJP party, volunteers & grass-root workers themselves. You are joining millions of users of this community who aspire for a New Telangana.

Here you can interact directly with Bandi Sanjay, contribute to party building through various ways and connect with karyakarthas. The Bandi Sanjay app keeps you updated with the initiatives and achievements of the government led by Bandi Sanjay. You can also connect with fellow volunteers and organise various events and initiatives.

Collection

To provide a tailor made and personalised experience, the information you share maybe processed and analysed by third party services who are engaged by us with due confidentiality obligations and solely for the specific purpose of such services. This will help the App give you the best possible experience by showing content in your language and according to your interests. For example, based on the location data collected, you may get a push notification when Bandi Sanjay is in your area or there is an important initiative or event happening in your area. Similarly, based on your previous interactions within the App you may receive push notifications based on your interests in the future.

Permissions

The Bandi Sanjay app requests you for several permissions in order to serve you better. All these permissions are contextual and cause-specific. You are also free to grant or deny many of these permissions. Below is a list of permissions requested along with the purpose they serve

Identity and Accounts: Permission to use your identity and use relevant information from your device to help you login to the App.

Camera and File Storage: Permission to access your camera and/or photos/media/files is requested to help you use features like ‘Selfie with Bandi Sanjay’, ‘Swachh Bharat’ and other such initiatives where posting a photo may be required to participate. These permissions are also required to enable you to post photos anywhere on the App.

Read SMS: Permission to access your SMS is used by user authentication module to read OTP sent over SMS to authenticate the user.

Microphone: Permission to access your microphone is requested only to enable you to upload/download the videos and images into the App. converse in live video facilities such as the feature on the Bandi Sanjay.

Wi-Fi/Network Connection/Network Access: Permission to access Wi-Fi, Network Connection and Network Access is taken only to allow the App to connect to the internet to provide its functionality.

Draw over other apps: Permission to draw over other apps is taken only to allow for a modern and contextual user interface that can be showcased outside the App.

The permissions asked for are used only for a specific feature or user action. For example, permission to use photos is used only when you want to post a photo and the file storage permission is used only to store the photo on the device.
